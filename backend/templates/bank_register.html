How Bank Onboarding Works

1

Register

Fill out the form below with your bank's details. You'll receive API credentials immediately.

2

Implement Bridge API

Build a POST endpoint that handles DEBIT, CREDIT, UNLOCK, and STATUS actions. See the Developer Docs for the full spec.

3

Admin Approval

An AtomicPay admin reviews your registration, runs connectivity tests, and approves your bank to go live.

4

Go Live

Once approved, your bank appears in the network and users can link their accounts and transact atomically.
